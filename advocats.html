Who are the AdvoCats?
Alumni, parents, students, community supporters, and everyone who wants to see a successful and strong university that is supported by the State of Arizona.
What do the AdvoCats do?
During the legislative session, it is imperative that the UA has a strong and effective voice to convince legislators of the need to support our policy agenda. UA administrators and government relations staff spend countrless hours meeting with senators and representatives and testifying to various committiees. The AdvoCats assist by working with the government relations staff to augment those efforts by meeting with legislators and the governor's staff, sending letters of support, making phone calls, and recruiting new AdvoCats.
